Cessna Skyhawk

Since 2021

Descripton

Cessna 172 Skyhawk (Skyhawk) - American light four-seater multipurpose aircraft, which is one of the most popular among the existing models of the aircraft manufacturer Cessna. Despite the fact that the development of this aircraft was carried out in the mid-50s of the last century, the production of the light-engine Cessna 172 Skyhawk aircraft continues to this day. Initially, the demand for production of the Cessna 172 Skyhawk was incredibly huge, however, closer to the mid-80s, consumer demand fell sharply, as a result of which production of this model was discontinued until 1998, after which production resumed. In total, over a 47-year period of production, more than 43 thousand units of these aircraft rolled off the assembly line of the American aircraft building company, which is why, at present, the Cessna 172 Skyhawk aircraft are the most massive in the history of world aviation.
