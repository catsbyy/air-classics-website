History

Air Classics is the outgrowth of Valerii’s lifelong fascination with planes and the sky. The atmosphere of biplanes and simple monoplanes cannot be compared with other modern aircraft, so we want to help people feel it. Our company is engaged in aircraft repair, rental and sale.

Successfully working as a pilot of international flights for an airline, I wanted to do my own business, closely related to airplanes. Since childhood, I liked classic designs, so I did not have to think long about the assortment. Classic designs and modern technologies allow you to completely immerse yourself in the ancient atmosphere, but at the same time be in modern comfort and safety.
